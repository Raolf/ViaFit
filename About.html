About us


ViaFit is a fitness center made and maintained by students at Via-Horsens.

location of the fitness center is Chr. M Østergaards Vej 4 8700 Horsens

ViaFit is open 24/7 and you can get access to the center with your Via Student card once you´ve signed up and payed!

ViaFit have different teams that you can join for free and you can join more than one team.

ViaFit offers Cardio,X-fit, fullbody and strength on a timed schedule from monday through sunday, you can find more information about it under Activities
